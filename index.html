Biography

I am a final-year doctoral student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas State University focusing on Eye-Tracking under the supervision of Dr. Oleg Komogortsev. My research revolves around eye tracking, eye-movement biometrics, and signal processing.

PhD Timeline

  • May 2025 - Our paper titled Evaluating Eye Movement Biometrics in Virtual Reality: A Comparative Analysis of VR Headset and High-End Eye-Tracker Performance has been accepted as a Short Paper in the IEEE SPW 2025.
  • March 2025 - Our paper, Evaluating Eye Tracking Signal Quality with Real-time Gaze Interaction Simulation: A Study Using an Offline Dataset has been accepted as a Short Paper in the ETRA 2025.
  • November 2024 - I have defended my dissertation proposal.

  • October 2023 - Our manuscript titled Determining Which Sine Wave Frequencies Correspond to Signal and Which Correspond to Noise in Eye-Tracking Time-Series got accepted in Journal of Eye Movement Research.
  • August 2023 - Our manuscript titled Filtering Eye-Tracking Data from an EyeLink 1000: Comparing Heuristic, Savitzky-Golay, IIR and FIR Digital Filters got accepted in Journal of Eye Movement Research.
